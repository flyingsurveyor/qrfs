1. Camera and photo

Collect chunks live or from single photos

Useful when some QR codes are read by an external app or only from a close-up photo.

?
The live camera collects chunks one at a time. The high-quality photo is decoded by the local backend with stronger preprocessing and can add one or more chunks from a single image. The chunks collected here are merged with the ones pasted manually or read from PDFs/images.
Tip: if the QR is folded or faded, try reading it very closely with the high-quality photo mode. That path uses the local backend instead of the browser decoder.
